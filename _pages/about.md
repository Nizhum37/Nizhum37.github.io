Welcome to my academic website.

I am **Dr. Nizhum Rahman**, a Postdoctoral Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan. My research lies at the intersection of applied mathematics, mathematical biology, computational modeling, and scientific computing.

My work focuses on developing and analyzing mathematical models to understand complex biological systems, including:

* Cancer–immune interactions
* Agent-based and hybrid computational models
* Collective cell migration
* Axonal transport and neurodegenerative diseases
* Mathematical oncology
* Machine learning integrated with mechanistic models

Prior to joining the University of Michigan, I completed my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at the University of Queensland, Australia. I also served as a Senior Lecturer in Mathematics at Daffodil International University, Bangladesh.
